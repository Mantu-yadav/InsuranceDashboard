# 🚗 Insurance Risk & Claims Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 1. 📌 Project Title / Headline

**Insurance Risk & Claims Analysis Dashboard**

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze insurance policies, claims, customer demographics, and vehicle information to help insurance companies identify risk patterns, monitor business performance, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 2. 📖 Short Description / Purpose

The **Insurance Risk & Claims Analysis Dashboard** provides a centralized reporting solution that transforms raw insurance policy and claims data into meaningful business insights.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor overall insurance performance.
- Track claim trends and financial impact.
- Analyze customer demographics.
- Identify high-risk customer segments.
- Compare policies and claims across different categories.
- Support strategic business decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

## 3. 🛠 Tech Stack

The project was built using the following technologies:

- **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard Development
- **Power Query** – Data Cleaning & Transformation
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI & Measure Calculations
- **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – Data Source
- **Data Modeling** – Relationship Management
- **Interactive Charts & Visualizations** – Business Reporting

---

## 4. 📂 Data Source

The dashboard uses an insurance dataset containing policyholder and claim information.

### Dataset includes:

- Policy ID
- Customer Name
- Gender
- Age
- Education
- Marital Status
- Car Make
- Car Year
- Car Use
- Coverage Zone
- Kids Driving
- Claim Amount
- Policy Details

The data was imported from **Excel/CSV files**, cleaned using **Power Query**, and modeled in **Power BI** for analysis.

## 5. ✨ Features / Highlights
